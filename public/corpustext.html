Explore new features of VOICE 3.0 Online

Enhanced search functions

In VOICE 3.0 Online, the previous interfaces VOICE Online and VOICE POS Online have been merged and refined. The new interface includes entirely new query options that make it possible to search for select mark-up of spoken language (pauses, laughter, overlaps, etc.). Queries for words and lexical tokens, POS, lemmas and mark-up can be flexibly combined and modified with different wildcards and regular expressions. For details see the VOICE 3.0 Online search manual.

Additional filter categories

VOICE 3.0 Online offers new filter categories for selecting speech events. In addition to domain and speech event type, you choose events and build your own subcorpus according to number of interactants/speakers, duration, number of words, and several other metadata categories.

New style options

VOICE 3.0 Online offers an enhanced and highly customizable display of search results and entire transcripts. Try out our style options (voice, plain, pos, xml) and activate/deactivate kwic style for in these different styles for search results. Adapt voice style according to your research needs by switching on/off the different tag groups (overlaps, pauses, etc.; new: e.g. laughter, uncertain transcription) with our 'flexi style' options.

Bookmarks and download

Try out the new bookmark and download function in VOICE 3.0 Online. Bookmarks can be locally stored at your own computer and can be ex-/imported. Search results and corpus texts can be downloaded in different file formats (.xlxs, .csv, .html, .txt). Downloaded files will mirroring the style options currently chosen in the online interface.
